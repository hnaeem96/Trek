About Trek

Trek is a minimalistic travel site that you can use to book flights and hotels to your favorite destinations, all around the world. Sign up today for the best booking experience you'll have!

Book with ease

Trek makes sure you have a smooth booking process, and an even smoother trip. Our team works closely with hotels and airliners to make sure this goal is always fulfilled.

Plan your trip

Use Trek's smart booking technology to help plan out your trip along the way. You'll be able to see the most optimal times to visit, and additional ways to save money.

Stay updated

Trek has tons of daily deals, and we'll make sure to notify you on sweet price-drops for any of the destinations you've been wanting to go to!

Earn points

The more you use Trek, the more we'll make sure you're rewarded for it. Earn reward points everytime you book a flight or hotel. The higher the status, the more savings for you!

How it works

1

Signup/Login to your Trek account, and search for where you want to go.

2

Choose your destination, and pick the best flight & hotel combination that fits your needs.

3

Go through our simple payment process, and you're all set! Sit tight till you fly out.
